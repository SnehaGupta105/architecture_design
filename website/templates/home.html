🏛️ Architecture & Civil Design

We create modern, functional and aesthetically pleasing architectural designs. Our focus is on proper ventilation, natural lighting, structural safety and efficient space utilization.

📐 Plot Layout & Blueprint

We prepare detailed plot layouts according to plot dimensions and client requirements. The blueprint includes room sizes, wall positions, doors, windows, staircases, and utility spaces.

These drawings help engineers, contractors, and workers understand the structure clearly and reduce mistakes during construction.
