About Gupta Building Designer

Crafting Smart, Modern & Vastu-Perfect Living Spaces

Building Dreams with Trust, Experience & Innovation

Since 2000, Gupta Building Designer has been dedicated to creating modern, functional, and Vastu-compliant architectural designs under the expert guidance of Er. Arvind Kumar Gupta. With over two decades of experience, the firm has earned a strong reputation for designing beautiful, practical, and cost-effective homes that perfectly match the needs and lifestyles of clients.

Over the years, we have successfully designed hundreds of residential homes across Banda and the surrounding areas, delivering innovative layouts, elegant elevations, and well-planned living spaces. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in residential building design.

Today, the legacy continues with Er. Harsh Gupta, a B.Tech Graduate in Civil Engineering, who combines technical expertise with modern architectural trends and advanced planning techniques. By blending years of experience with fresh ideas and the latest design concepts, we ensure that every project reflects excellence, functionality, and timeless beauty.

At Gupta Building Designer, we believe that every home is more than just a building—it's a dream, a lifelong investment, and a place where memories are created. Our mission is to transform your vision into reality through creative design, precise planning, and personalized service.
