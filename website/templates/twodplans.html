2D Floor Plans

A 2D floor plan is a detailed drawing of a house layout viewed from above. It shows the arrangement of rooms, doors, windows, kitchen, washroom, staircase, and other important areas.

Why is a 2D Plan Important?

  • Helps understand room arrangement.
  • Proper space utilization.
  • Easy communication with engineers and builders.
  • Reduces construction mistakes.
  • Provides clear measurements before construction.

Our Process

  1. Collect plot dimensions.
  2. Understand client requirements.
  3. Create layout and room placement.
  4. Add doors, windows, staircase and utilities.
  5. Finalize the plan with client approval.
